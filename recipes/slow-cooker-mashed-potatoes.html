• 5 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes
  • 2 cups Parmesan cheese
  • 1 ½ cups cream cheese
  • 1 cup chopped fresh chives
  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ medium head garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste
  1. 1st Step

    2. Gather all ingredients./p>

  2. 2nd Step

    3. Peel and quarter potatoes; place in a large pot. Cover with salted water and bring to a boil. Cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes.

  3. 3rd Step

    4. Drain and return potatoes to the pot over low heat to dry for 1 to 2 minutes.

  4. 4th Step

    5. Add Parmesan cheese, cream cheese, chives, butter, garlic, salt, and pepper.

  5. 5th Step

    6. Use a potato masher to mash until smooth and serve.
